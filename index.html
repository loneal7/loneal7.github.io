About Us

Patriot is a veteran founded company since 2004 that serves the greater Clarksville area including Montgomery, Davidson and Stewart Counties in TN and the Christian and Trigg county areas in KY.
We provide mobile cleaning services for residential, commercial, and industrial spaces utilizing van and truck mounted water vacuum systems. Our services and cleanings consist of tile, upholstery, carpet, odor, and water damage. Patriot provides water damage and removal service for external flooding, plumbing failures and other water issues. We pre-treat stains with environmentally safe products that remove discoloration, dirt, fluids, and most stains from day to day living. We can give an immediate quote through 931-801-0865 and/or patriotcarpets@gmail.com.
We are available 24 hours for emergency services and 7:30am-5pm during the week.





Services

Area Rug Cleaning

We offer full-service area rug cleaning. This process is similar to having your carpets cleaned. We bring the equipment to you.

Carpet Cleaning & Grooming

We clean any type of material using our specialized machines. We also offer carpet grooming which helps to lift the fabric up as a free bonus. We recommend having your carpets cleaned every six months.

Water Damage Cleaning

We offer full-service water damage cleaning and aim to dry the area within 72 hours. We can work directly with your insurance company depending on your level of coverage.

Ceramic Tile & Grout Cleaning

Ceramic tile and grout cleaning is available. This gives the surface a cleaner look and pulls the dirt out of floor. We recommend having your tile and grout professionally cleaned once a year.

Furniture & Upholstery Cleaning

We provide full-service furniture and upholstery cleaning. We recommend adding Scotchgard to prevent stains after the cleaning for an additional fee.

Auto, Boat, and RV Cleaning

We offer full-service auto, boat and RV cleaning services. This service can take anywhere from 30 minutes to two hours based on the size and condition of the vehicle.
